Precinct Master Plan 1

The fundamental principle of the Campus Master Plan is to combine SoM, FoD and FoS with NUH at master plan level to realize the formation of an Academic Medical Centre (AMC) that synergizes Clinics, Translational Research, Teaching and Multi-disciplinary Basic Sciences in the hope of creating an environment where new knowledge is founded, new treatment is provided and new expert-scientists are trained.
